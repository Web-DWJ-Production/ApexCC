About Us

Since 1991, Apex Communications Corporation (ACC) has operated with the express purpose of being a quality, innovative and profitable Program Management and Services organization. We are an SBA Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB) and SBA Certified Small Business and pride ourselves on making prudent and effective use of products, training, personnel and strategic partnerships.
